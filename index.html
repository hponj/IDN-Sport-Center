WHAT IS THE IDN BOARDING SCHOOL SPORTS AREA?

IDN Boarding School Sports Club is a sports activity platform located within the IDN Boarding School, an educational institution based on Islamic boarding school principles and information technology, located in Pamijahan, Bogor. This club is designed to support the physical, mental, and character development of students through structured and competitive sports activities.
